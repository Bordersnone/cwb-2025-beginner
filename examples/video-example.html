Example 3: Video with Custom Attributes

You can control video behavior with attributes:

Attributes explained:

  • controls - Shows play/pause buttons
  • autoplay - Starts playing automatically
  • muted - Starts with sound off (required for autoplay on most browsers)
  • loop - Repeats the video when it ends
  • poster="image.jpg" - Shows a thumbnail before playing

Tips for Using Videos

  • Keep video file sizes small (compress them if needed)
  • Always provide alternative text or descriptions for accessibility
  • Use YouTube or other hosting services for large videos
  • Be mindful of copyright - only use videos you have permission to use
  • Test your videos in different browsers
